About me

Bio

I’m a game developer from Brazil who started playing video games really early in life and after having the opportunity to play Ultima Online despite my young age at that time, the hobby became a passion.

After I graduated as a Digital Games Technologist at Universidade do Vale do Rio dos Sinos I’ve developed and published a few Flash games on my own and out of those, my most successful one was Blockage, which was played by well over 10 million players and was well received in the Flash gaming community.

I wasn’t too confident with the brazilian game development market at the time and I felt it was time to move abroad.

Not too long after arriving in Ireland in 2014 I joined WarDucks. Since then I’ve helped the company successfuly launch over 7 titles on multiple platforms.
