Description

Space Draw is a fun game set in space. It all happens very fast: you’re in space, you’re an astronaut and, oops, you’re falling.

You control the astronaut movement by drawing on the screen. Just remember to avoid aliens and reach for the stars

It’s up to you: How far will you travel? How many stars will you collect? How long will it take until we have to call Houston?

And when you die, don’t worry. You won’t feel a thing. Just touch the screen again and you’re back in your space suit. So what are you waiting for? Challenge your friends and Space Draw!
