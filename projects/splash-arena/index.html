Description

Splash Arena is a new take on the old bubble shooting formula.

Now you can challenge your friends and see who’s the baddest, meanest and fastest shooter around. You can play it in groups of 2 to 4 people on your device.

Gather around and put your thumb to work. Try to match 3 bubbles of the same color to get ahead.

And don’t forget: great aim is a must, but you better be quick. The clock is ticking and there’s a crazy cow in the way.
